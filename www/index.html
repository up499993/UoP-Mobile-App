Useful Info

This page contains useful information for students about the services and facilities provided by the University of Portsmouth. Click on a department to show more information about it.

Academic Skills Unit

The Academic Skills Unit (ASK) can help you to understand and develop your thinking, writing and organisational skills through confidential tuition, workshops and a wide range of handouts and resources.

ASK can be found on the Third Floor of The Nuffield Centre or can be contacted on +44 (0)23 9284 3028 or academicskills@port.ac.uk

Opening Times

Monday - Thursday: 8:30am - 5.00pm
Fridays: 8.30am - 4.00pm

What We Offer

One-to-one tuition

Confidential tuition personalised for all campus-based or distance learners, with evening sessions available.

Our friendly, non-judgmental and highly experienced lecturers offer confidential tuition that is personalised for each individual student. Through discussion and reflection on previous academic work, we help students:

  • improve their understanding of the kinds of thinking and writing that foster success
  • discover practical steps for improving academic performance

Students may refer themselves to ASK (though they may be recommended to do so by a subject lecturer or personal tutor). For campus-based students, tutorials are typically one hour long and are held in the ASK offices in the Nuffield Centre. The number of tutorials and precise focus will vary according to need and are always negotiated and agreed with the student.

Distance-learning students may be supported by telephone, e-mail or other software, as agreed by the tutor and student once initial contact has been made.

Evening tutorials are offered between 5pm and 7pm on most Wednesday evenings in term time.

Resources

You can have access to a wide selection of resources including an online study skills resource, as well as a variety of handouts available in hard copy at the Nuffield Centre, the University Library and other locations around the campus. Handouts can also be downloaded from the Academic Skills Unit website here.

Online Resources

Skills4Study Campus from Palgrave Macmillan is the University of Portsmouth's online study skills resource. Its modules cover all of your essential study skills needs. You can access it from the Moodle course 'skills@portsmouth'.

Workshops

We offer an extensive programme of workshops, each of which addresses a particular theme or skill. Our experienced lecturers use a mix of teaching, discussion and activity to develop your understanding and skills.

Students who have attended these workshops have commented on the 'approachable tutors', 'excellent delivery' and 'useful insight' that 'really made me think' and 'made me feel like I can gain marks'.

It is a great way to tackle any study-related issues and brush up on your academic skills to hopefully gain the results you wish to achieve.
